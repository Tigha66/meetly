---

## One-Page Business Plan

### Positioning

**What we sell:** WhatsApp AI Receptionist for local businesses.
**What we don't sell:** A chatbot tool.

The pitch is outcome-based:
> "Every missed WhatsApp enquiry is a lost customer. Our AI receptionist answers instantly — day or night — captures the lead, qualifies the request, and hands off to you when it matters. One extra booking pays for the month."

### Target Niches (in order)

1. Cleaning companies
2. Barbers / salons / nail techs
3. Mobile mechanics / garages
4. Plumbers / electricians / roofers
5. Estate agents / letting agents

Avoid: dental/medical clinics (regulated — add after testimonials exist).

### Risk Warnings (read before selling)

- **OpenWA is for demos and pilots only.** It uses unofficial WhatsApp Web automation. It carries account-ban risk if abused.
- **Production clients should use the official WhatsApp Cloud API** where possible. Especially if their WhatsApp number is critical to their business.
- **No spam. No scraped lists. No cold bulk WhatsApp blasting.**
- **Use opt-in/customer-initiated messages only.**
- For UK/EU clients: GDPR basics apply (privacy notice, data processor agreement, retention policy, delete on request).

### Offer & Pricing

| Package | UK | US |
|---|---|---|
| **Starter** — FAQ replies, hours, services/prices, lead capture, quote/booking flow, human handoff, basic monthly report, hosting/monitoring, 1 round of monthly updates | £299 setup + £99/mo | $399 setup + $149/mo |
| **Pro** — Everything in Starter + advanced quote/booking workflows, CRM/Google Sheets integration, email/WhatsApp lead alerts to owner, conversation summaries, weekly/monthly lead report, monthly optimization | £699 setup + £199/mo | $899 setup + $299/mo |
| **Managed** — Full setup + management, multi-location support, advanced handoff, integrations, analytics, priority support | £1,000–£2,500 setup + £300–£750/mo | $1,500–$3,000 setup + $499–$999/mo |

**ROI line for every sale:** "One extra job, booking, or quote can pay for the monthly fee."

### Delivery Model

- **Demo/Pilot:** OpenWA (fast setup, no Meta approval needed).
- **Production:** Official WhatsApp Cloud API (safe, scalable, compliant).
- **Dashboard:** Vercel.
- **Backend:** Hostinger VPS at `76.13.252.4` (temporary API domain: `api.76-13-252-4.sslip.io`).

### First Revenue Goal

**Get 1 paying client at £99/month or $149/month.**
Then 5 clients. Then productize onboarding. Then increase pricing.

## Lead Scoring Template

### Scoring Criteria (0 or 1 each)

| # | Criteria | Score 1 if... |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | WhatsApp or Instagram DM publicly visible | Yes — listed on website/social |
| 2 | Depends on bookings or quote requests | Yes — core business model |
| 3 | Gets repetitive questions | Yes — evidence in reviews/social |
| 4 | No chatbot or poor automation | Yes — no bot detected |
| 5 | Slow replies or reviews mentioning communication problems | Yes — evidence found |
| 6 | High-value service (one booking covers monthly fee) | Yes — pricing supports it |

**Score interpretation:**
- 5–6 = High priority (contact first)
- 3–4 = Medium priority
- 0–2 = Low priority

## Objection Handling

### "I already use WhatsApp"

> "That's great — it means your customers already prefer it. This isn't about replacing WhatsApp, it's about making sure every message gets a fast reply. Even the best businesses miss messages when they're busy with clients. The assistant handles the routine stuff so you can focus on the conversations that need you."

### "I don't need AI"

> "Totally fair. Most businesses don't 'need' it until they count how many enquiries slip through. Can I ask — have you ever missed a WhatsApp and found out later a customer went elsewhere? That's usually the wake-up call."

### "Too expensive"

> "I get it. Here's how I think about it: one extra job from a captured lead pays for the whole month. If the assistant captures even one booking you'd have otherwise missed, it's already paid for itself. Would it help to start with a short pilot so you can see the ROI before committing?"

### "Will this spam customers?"

> "No. The assistant only replies to messages that customers send first. We never send unsolicited messages. It's purely a better way to respond to the enquiries you're already getting."

### "Will my WhatsApp get banned?"

> "For demos and short pilots, we use a lightweight setup that runs locally. For ongoing production clients, we recommend the official WhatsApp Cloud API — it's Meta-approved and carries no ban risk. We'll recommend the right approach based on your situation."

### "Can I try it first?"

> "Absolutely. We can set up a free 7–14 day pilot using your business. You'll see exactly how it works, how many leads it captures, and you decide. No commitment."

## Security & Compliance Reminders

- **Do not recommend spam or bulk WhatsApp messaging.**
- **Do not scrape private data.**
- **Do not store more customer data than needed.**
- **Use opt-in or customer-initiated messaging only.**
- **Keep human handoff available at all times.**
- **Keep logs minimal and secure.**
- **Never expose, print, or log full API keys.**
- **Never commit `.env`, API keys, QR codes, WhatsApp session files, SQLite DB files, logs, browser cache, `node_modules`, or build output.**
- **For UK/EU clients:** mention GDPR basics — privacy notice, data processor agreement, retention policy, delete on request.
- **For larger clients:** recommend official WhatsApp Cloud API.
